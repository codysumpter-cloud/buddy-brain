# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors should communicate clearly, respectfully, and in good faith.

We expect:

- respectful discussion
- specific technical feedback
- honest reporting of risk, uncertainty, and failure
- professional handling of disagreement

We do not accept:

- harassment or personal attacks
- intimidation
- doxxing or exposing private information
- knowingly deceptive claims about tests, fixes, or system state

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If conduct becomes unsafe or disruptive, report it privately to the maintainer using the private path referenced in `SECURITY.md`.
